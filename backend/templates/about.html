ABOUT US

OUR HISTORY

The association of Professional Women Engineers of Nigeria APWEN
was formed by a handful of women engineers led by Engr. (Mrs.) J.O Maduka in 1982 and was formally inaugurated
in 1983. It was originally meant to be a pressure group since most men hiring engineers then would rather have women
in the kitchen and not on construction site or behind the desk carrying out engineering designs.



OUR MISSION

To continously increase awareness that engineering is a career for girls also, thereby
improving the numerical strength of females engineers.



OUR VISION

be the catalyst for the advancement of women in the engineering profession
towards national and global technological developmen.t



OUR OBJECTIVES

To provide a forum for promoting soronity amongst female engineers.



OUR WORK

Since 1982 APWEN has recorded a level of achievement of her objective by advancing professionals knowledge
of members through:
Facilitation of continuing profesional development workshops
organising industrial visitsvto engineering facilities
career counselling sessions for female students in Primary,Secondary and Tertiary institutions.

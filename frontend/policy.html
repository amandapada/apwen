APWEN ABU COLLEGIATE GENERAL POLICIES.


MEMBERSHIP Eligibility:

  • Membership shall be open to all female engineering students pursuing a degree in any field of Engineering at Ahmadu Bello University.
  • from Polytechnics shall be registered as Associate Collegiate Members and may participate in activities but shall not have voting rights.

Membership Rights

1. Upon payment of the prescribed registration fee, student members shall be entitled to vote and be voted for in Collegiate elections (subject to eligibility criteria).

2. Members shall have the right to participate in all activities and events organized by the Collegiate.


ELIGIBILITY TO HOLD OFFICE:
  • General:
  • To hold any elective or appointive office within the Collegiate, a member must:
  • 1. Be a registered member in good standing;
  • A member in good standing is one who:
    1. 1. Actively participates in Collegiate and National activities.
    2. 2. Has attended at least 50% of meetings and activities in the year preceding the election.
  • No member shall be elected as an officer of the Collegiate unless she has been a member in good standing for at least one year and has attended at least one National Conference.
  • 2. Be a student of the School/Faculty of Engineering at Ahmadu Bello University.

    • Management Structure

  • 1. The Executive Committee shall oversee the administration of the Collegiate.
  • 2. All members of the Executive Committee are mandated to attend the National Conference.

    • Financial Transparency

  • 1. All Collegiate funds shall be deposited into an officially recognized bank account.
  • Under no circumstance shall Collegiate funds be collected or deposited into a personal account.
